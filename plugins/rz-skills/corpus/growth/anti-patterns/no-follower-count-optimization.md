# Don't Optimize for Follower Count Over Engagement Quality

## What it is
The rule: don't optimize for follower count. Optimize for engagement quality — substantive comments from senior PMs, reshares from 1K+ accounts, peer DMs, advisory inquiries. A 2K-follower account whose audience is senior PMs at AI companies is more valuable than a 10K-follower account whose audience is junior PMs and bots.

## Why it matters
Follower count is a vanity metric (`corpus/growth/metrics/anti-vanity-metrics.md`) that drives the wrong behavior. Optimizing for it produces engagement-bait posts that inflate count and degrade Segment 2 trust. Optimizing for engagement quality produces posts that compound into peer relationships, speaker invitations, and recruiter DMs. The tradeoff is clear: count flatters; quality compounds.

## How to apply
- **Refuse follower-count tactics:** engagement-bait questions ("agree?"), polls without substance, "share if you agree" CTAs, viral hooks with thin substance.
- **Track quality first, count second.** The dashboard (`corpus/growth/metrics/dashboard-template.md`) leads with senior-PM comments and reshares from 1K+ accounts; follower count is context only.
- **Don't post follower-count screenshots.** Even when growth is real, "just hit 5K!" reads as performative and signals vanity-optimization.
- **When asked "how many followers do you have?"** answer with the underlying signal: "I'm not optimizing for follower count — what I track is Segment 2 reshares and peer DMs, and those are up 40% this quarter."
- **Set a quality floor.** Posts must pass the senior-PM-comment test (would a senior PM bother to comment?) before they post.

## Examples
- A peer asks "how do I get to 10K followers?" Riché reframes: "What's your reshare count from 1K+ accounts? That's the lever, not follower count."
- LinkedIn shows a viral post pulled 50K impressions but only 2 substantive senior-PM comments. Logged as "high vanity, low quality" — pattern to avoid.
- A Wednesday deep dive on context architecture pulls 3K impressions but 4 reshares from 2K+ follower senior PMs. Logged as "low vanity, high quality" — pattern to repeat.
- A "best of" PM list places Riché on it; follower count jumps 800 in a week. Most of the new followers are junior PMs and bots; quality stays flat. Riché doesn't celebrate the count spike.
