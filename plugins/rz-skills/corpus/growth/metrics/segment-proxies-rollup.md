# Segment Proxies — Rollup of All Four Segments

## What it is
The consolidated table of leading-indicator proxies across all four audience segments — 16 proxies total, 4 per segment. The standalone segment-metrics entries cover each in detail. This entry is the rollup view used in weekly dashboard review.

## Why it matters
Tracking each segment separately is necessary; tracking only one obscures whether the flywheel is balanced. If Segment 2 is strong but Segment 4 is silent, the speaker pitches need attention. If Segment 1 is flat but Segment 2 is climbing, the timing is normal. The rollup makes the imbalances visible in one view.

## How to apply
The 16 proxies, by segment:

**Segment 1 — AI Product Hiring Managers:**
- Inbound recruiter DMs
- Profile views from AI company employees
- "How did you hear about me" traces
- Mentions in hiring conversations (anecdotal)

**Segment 2 — Product Leader Peers (PRIMARY):**
- Comment engagement from senior PMs
- Reshares from people with 1,000+ followers
- DM conversations initiated by peers
- ProductTank connections and follow-ups

**Segment 3 — Founders Building AI Products:**
- DMs asking for architecture advice
- Advisory inquiries
- X engagement from founder accounts
- Mentions in accelerator Slacks

**Segment 4 — Event Organizers and Podcast Hosts:**
- Speaking invitations (inbound)
- CFP acceptances
- Podcast guest requests
- Event organizer DMs

Track all 16 weekly. The dashboard template (`corpus/growth/metrics/dashboard-template.md`) has slots for each.

## Examples
- Q1 2026 rollup view:
  - S1: 4 recruiter DMs, 6 AI-company profile views, 2 traced
  - S2: 18 senior-PM comments, 7 reshares from 1K+ accounts, 12 peer DMs, 3 ProductTank connections
  - S3: 4 founder DMs, 1 advisory inquiry, 9 X founder engagements, 1 accelerator Slack mention
  - S4: 1 inbound speaking invitation, 1 CFP acceptance, 1 podcast booking, 2 organizer DMs
- Imbalance diagnosis: S2 strong, S4 weak. Action: increase Segment 4 outreach (CFP submissions for next two events), use Segment 2 referral pull.

## Anti-patterns
- Tracking only one segment. The flywheel needs all four; gaps compound.
- Mixing leading and lagging in the same view. Keep the rollup leading-only; lagging goes to the quarterly review.
