# Weekly Dashboard Template

## What it is
The format for the weekly metrics dashboard, updated in the 15-min Friday admin block. Tracks the 16 segment proxies, weekly post performance, and pipeline state. Lives wherever Riché tracks data (Notion, spreadsheet, etc.) — this is the schema, not the tool.

## Why it matters
Without a structured weekly dashboard, the metrics ritual fails. People skip "checking metrics" because there's no clear thing to check. The template makes the 15 min predictable and forcing-functions honest measurement. After 12 weeks of consistent dashboard updates, patterns emerge that wouldn't be visible from intuition alone.

## How to apply

**Dashboard sections:**

**1. This week's posts (5 rows, one per post day Mon-Fri):**
- Date | Content type | Topic | Likes | Comments | Reshares | Reshares from 1K+ | Senior-PM comments

**2. Segment 1 proxies:**
- Inbound recruiter DMs this week (count + named accounts)
- AI-company profile views this week (count + named viewers from LinkedIn Premium)
- New "how did you hear about me" traces (logged with origin)

**3. Segment 2 proxies:**
- Senior-PM comments received (rolled up from post table)
- Reshares from 1K+ accounts (rolled up)
- Peer DMs initiated by peers (count + names)
- New ProductTank/event connections (count + names)

**4. Segment 3 proxies:**
- Founder DMs received (count)
- Advisory inquiries (count + status)
- Founder X engagement (named accounts)
- Accelerator Slack mentions (anecdotal log)

**5. Segment 4 proxies:**
- Speaking invitations received (with venue)
- CFP submissions sent / received outcomes
- Podcast pitches sent / received outcomes
- Event organizer DMs

**6. Time budget audit:**
- Hours spent on each playbook activity (commenting, DMs, writing, X, admin)
- Total: target 5.25 hrs/week. Overrun? Note cause.

**7. Notes / patterns observed:**
- 2–3 lines on what worked, what didn't, what to try next week.

## Examples
- Week of June 17–21: 3 posts (Mon hot take, Wed deep dive on context architecture, Fri Suzy launch story). Wed deep dive pulled 3 reshares from 2K+ accounts and 4 senior-PM comments. S1: 1 recruiter DM, 2 AI-company profile views (named in LinkedIn Premium). S2: 4 peer DMs, 2 ProductTank connections. S4: 1 podcast pitch reply (declined), 1 CFP submitted to AI Product Summit. Time budget: 5.5 hrs (slight overrun on commenting). Note: Wed deep-dive structure to repeat; ProductTank attendance compounding nicely.
- A bad-data week: dashboard skipped. Following week: two weeks of patterns hidden. Discipline matters.

## Anti-patterns
- Skipping weeks. The compounding insight requires consistent data.
- Adding 30 metrics. The 16 segment proxies + posts + time + notes is enough; more dilutes signal.
