# Website SEO Targets

## What it is
The SEO keyword targets for richezamor.com:
- "context architecture"
- "AI context management"
- "context generation AI"

Plus secondary targets: "AI product manager hiring," "context layer for AI products," "retrieval architecture AI PM," "context engineering for AI products."

## Why it matters
SEO is the asynchronous channel that complements the active flywheel. When a Segment 1 hiring manager or Segment 4 event organizer searches the keywords, ranking on page 1 of Google means Riché is found without active outreach. The keyword choice matters: "AI product manager" is too competitive (millions of pages); "context architecture" is positioned (low competition, high intent).

## How to apply
- **Anchor every long-form piece on richezamor.com to one of the SEO targets.** Page title, H1, meta description.
- **Use LinkedIn articles for early ranking.** LinkedIn's domain authority means articles rank faster than personal site content. Migrate to richezamor.com after 60–90 days for permanent equity.
- **Internal linking:** every article links to the Context Layer Engine demo and the speaker one-sheet. Strengthens both ranking and conversion.
- **Don't chase generic keywords.** "AI product management" has high volume and zero conversion; "context architecture" has low volume and 100% conversion (visitors are already qualified).
- **Track via Google Search Console.** Quarterly review of rankings and click-through.

## Examples
- An article published on richezamor.com titled "What is Context Architecture in AI Products?" targets the primary keyword. Within 6 months, ranks position 3 on Google for "context architecture AI." Visitors are mostly senior PMs and founders — exactly Segments 2 and 3.
- A LinkedIn article on "AI Context Management for Product Teams" ranks position 5 within 3 weeks (LinkedIn domain authority). After 90 days, Riché republishes to richezamor.com; the personal site picks up the ranking over the next 6 months.
- A Segment 4 event organizer searches "AI product context architecture speaker." Riché's site ranks position 2; speaker one-sheet is one click. Inbound speaking inquiry follows.
