# X Target Accounts (9)

## What it is
The 9 X accounts that anchor Riché's X engagement: @shreaborhade, @lennysan, @joulee, @johncutlefish, @sachinrekhi, @caborojo, @gibsonbiddle, @lissijean, @ttorres.

The structured per-account data (focus area, post archetype) lives in `corpus/growth/target-accounts/x.yaml`. This entry covers the rationale and the comparison with the LinkedIn list.

## Why it matters
The X list is smaller than the LinkedIn list (9 vs 18) for two reasons: (1) X engagement budget is smaller (15 min/day vs 15–20), and (2) some PM thinkers are LinkedIn-primary and barely active on X (Cagan, Perri's main work is LinkedIn). The 9-account X list is the subset with active X presence. Plus founder accounts that aren't on the LinkedIn list (the founder side of Segment 3 reach).

## How to apply
- **Daily scan in the 15 min X block.** Pick 1–2 from the 9 to engage substantively. Don't try to engage all 9 daily.
- **Match the post archetype.** Some accounts post threads; others post short reactions. See `corpus/growth/target-accounts/x.yaml` for archetype per account.
- **Quote-tweet over retweet.** Always add perspective — see `corpus/growth/channels/x-quote-tweet-pattern.md`.
- **The X list complements the LinkedIn list.** A target on both lists (Lenny, Shreyas, John Cutler) gets cross-platform engagement. Different angles for different platforms.

The 9 accounts:

1. **@shreaborhade** (Shreyas Doshi) — PM craft, frameworks. Threads or reactions.
2. **@lennysan** (Lenny Rachitsky) — PM newsletter cross-posts, reactions.
3. **@joulee** (Julie Zhuo) — design + product leadership. Threads.
4. **@johncutlefish** (John Cutler) — team metrics, operating models.
5. **@sachinrekhi** (Sachin Rekhi) — product strategy, cross-functional.
6. **@caborojo** (Carlos Borojo / Aakash) — AI PM specifically.
7. **@gibsonbiddle** (Gibson Biddle) — Netflix-era PM, frameworks.
8. **@lissijean** (Melissa Perri) — product leadership, podcast cross-posts.
9. **@ttorres** (Teresa Torres) — discovery, OSTs.

## Examples
- A Shreyas X thread on PM frameworks. Riché quote-tweets: "What's missing for AI PMs is that 'framework selection' changes when context architecture changes." Quote-tweet pulls a reply from @caborojo.
- A Teresa Torres OST thread. Riché replies in-thread with practitioner extension. Two senior PMs reply.
- A Sachin Rekhi take on product strategy. Riché quote-tweets with the context-architecture angle. Strong founder pickup.
