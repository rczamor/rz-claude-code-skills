# Recorded Content Strategy

## What it is
The workflow for turning a single talk recording into 6+ months of derivative content. Specifically: 6–10 LinkedIn clips (30–90s each), 1–2 X threads, 1–2 long-form articles, 1 audio segment for podcast pitches, and reusable visual assets (slides, frameworks).

## Why it matters
A talk delivered to 200 people is a 200-person event. A talk recorded and recycled is a 20,000+ person event over 6 months. The leverage multiple is 100x — and the marginal cost is ~5–10 hours of editing/repurposing. This is the highest-ROI work in the entire growth system once a talk has happened.

## How to apply

**Editing (within 14 days of the talk):**
- Get a clean recording (request from organizer if not self-captured).
- Clip 6–10 standalone segments of 30–90 seconds. Each clip is one core idea, framework, or anecdote.
- Add captions (LinkedIn auto-plays muted; captions are required).
- Format for vertical/square aspect ratio for LinkedIn.

**Distribution (over 6+ months):**
- **LinkedIn:** post 1 clip per week as part of the Mon-Fri content rotation. Each clip post is a Wednesday deep dive variant or a Friday story. See `corpus/content-system/`.
- **X:** assemble 1–2 threads pulling key clips and quotes. See `corpus/growth/channels/x-thread-format.md`.
- **Articles:** write 1–2 long-form pieces on richezamor.com that expand on the talk's frameworks. SEO-targeted.
- **Podcast pitches:** the audio segment is the "preview clip" that closes new podcast pitches.

**Iteration:**
- Track which clips pull strongest engagement. Patterns reveal which moments of the talk resonated. Use those for the next talk's structure.

## Examples
- ProductTank NYC talk on context architecture (June 22): 30-min talk recorded.
  - 8 LinkedIn clips over 8 weeks (Wednesday deep-dive replacements + new Tuesday signal posts)
  - A 6-tweet X thread on the 5-step diagnostic
  - A long-form article on richezamor.com targeting "context architecture for AI products" SEO
  - A 5-min preview clip used in 4 podcast pitches over 60 days
  - The slide deck reformatted as a downloadable framework PDF on the speaker page
- A clip on "the diagnostic question most AI PMs skip" pulls 3 reshare from senior PMs in week 2. Pattern: diagnostic-style clips outperform anecdote clips for Segment 2.

## Anti-patterns
- Letting a recording sit untouched for 60+ days. Half-life of "fresh content" matters; clip and schedule within 2 weeks.
- Publishing all 8 clips in one week. Spread over 8 weeks for sustained reach.
