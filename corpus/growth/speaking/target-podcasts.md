# Target Podcasts

## What it is
The priority list of podcasts for Segment 4 outreach:

1. **Lenny's Podcast** (Lenny Rachitsky) — Tier 1, Segment 1/2/3 audience, highest-leverage single booking.
2. **Product Thinking** (Melissa Perri) — Tier 1, senior PM and CPO audience.
3. **Shreyas Doshi's podcast** — Tier 1 if guest-driven, otherwise reaction-only.
4. **The Product Podcast** (Product School) — Tier 2, broad PM audience, frequent cadence.
5. **SVPG / Product Therapy** (Marty Cagan and team) — Tier 1, opinionated leadership audience.
6. **All Things Product** (Teresa Torres & Petra Wille) — Tier 2, discovery + leadership focus.

Plus secondary tier: AI-specific PM podcasts (a16z, Latent Space when relevant), internal-leadership podcasts at Forbes AI 50 companies.

## Why it matters
Podcasts are the highest-trust channel for Segment 1 (hiring managers) — VPs and CPOs listen to Lenny and Cagan during commutes. A single Lenny's Podcast booking can do more for Segment 1 reach than 6 months of LinkedIn posts. The leverage is asymmetric: an hour of recording compounds into peer reshares, derivative posts, and credibility for the next year of pitches.

## How to apply
- **Tier 1 first.** Lenny's, Product Thinking, SVPG. Use `corpus/growth/speaking/podcast-pitch-template.md`.
- **Pitch with a specific angle for that show.** Lenny's audience is generalist PM; angle: AI PM context architecture as hidden moat. Cagan's audience is leadership; angle: the practitioner case for context architecture.
- **Use Segment 2 referrals.** Most podcast bookings come through peer recommendation, not cold pitch. Build Segment 2 first.
- **Repurpose every podcast appearance.** Audio → LinkedIn clips, deep-dive blog post, X thread. See `corpus/growth/flywheel/recorded-content-fuel.md`.
- **Track conversion.** Pitches sent, acceptances, derivative content count.

## Examples
- Lenny's Podcast booker reaches out via Segment 2 peer who reshared Riché's deep dive. Pitch references the live demo capability and the Helm Labs $3.25M pipeline. Booked.
- A pitch to Product Thinking emphasizes the practitioner-not-theorist angle relevant to Melissa Perri's leadership audience. Suzy launch + Helm Labs metrics close the credibility check.
- SVPG / Product Therapy invitation comes through a peer connection at a Reforge event. The pitch references Cagan's discovery frame and how it adapts to context-architecture-driven AI products.
- A mid-tier PM podcast booking yields 6 LinkedIn clips and 1 X thread in derivative content over 60 days.

## Anti-patterns
- Pitching all 6 with the same boilerplate. Each show has a specific audience and host frame; tailor.
- Skipping derivative content from podcast appearances. The audio is the asset; not reusing it wastes the appearance.
