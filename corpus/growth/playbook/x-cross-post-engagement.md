# X Cross-Post and Engagement (15 min/day)

## What it is
15 minutes per day on X: adapt the LinkedIn batch posts for X format and engage with the 9 X target accounts (@shreaborhade, @lennysan, @joulee, @johncutlefish, @sachinrekhi, @caborojo, @gibsonbiddle, @lissijean, @ttorres) and adjacent founder/PM accounts.

## Why it matters
X is the secondary channel that reaches Segment 3 (founders) and a different slice of Segment 2 (X-native PMs and product thinkers). Without X presence, Segment 3 advisory inquiries stay near zero, and content reach is bounded by LinkedIn's audience. The 15 min/day budget is small but consistent — that's enough to maintain X presence without bloating the time budget.

## How to apply
- **Adapt LinkedIn content for X format.** Wednesday LinkedIn deep dive becomes Tuesday X thread (5–7 tweets). See `corpus/growth/channels/x-thread-format.md`.
- **Engage with the 9 target accounts daily.** Quote-tweet with added perspective (never just retweet — see `corpus/growth/channels/x-quote-tweet-pattern.md`). 1–2 substantive replies per day.
- **Lean into founder hooks.** Practitioner-not-theorist plays harder on X than on LinkedIn. Lead with the live demo URL, specific metrics, technical depth.
- **Don't be on X for 90 minutes.** The discipline is the budget. 15 min in, 15 min out.
- **Track engagement separately from LinkedIn.** Different audience; different signals. See `corpus/growth/segments/segment-3-metrics.md`.

## Examples
- Tuesday morning: Wednesday's LinkedIn deep dive on context architecture is adapted into a 6-tweet thread. Posted on X. Two senior PMs and one founder quote-tweet during the day. Follow-ups happen in the next day's 15 min block.
- Riché quote-tweets a @shreaborhade post on PM frameworks with: "What's missing for AI PMs is that the 'context architecture' layer changes which framework applies. Discovery looks different when the user can't articulate the context they need." Quote-tweet adds perspective.
- A YC founder DMs after seeing Riché's X thread on retrieval. The DM gets a same-day reply (within the 15 min block). Conversion to advisory inquiry.

- Just retweeting target accounts. Quote-tweets with perspective; never bare retweets.
