# Segment 3 Metrics

## What it is
The four leading-indicator proxies for Segment 3 (AI Founders) reach:

1. **DMs asking for architecture advice** — founders reaching out to discuss retrieval, context, evals, or AI product strategy.
2. **Advisory inquiries** — founders or accelerators asking about formal advisory roles.
3. **X engagement from founder accounts** — quote tweets, replies, or DMs from accounts that read as founder (bio mentions company, accelerator, building).
4. **Mentions in accelerator Slacks** — anecdotal but tracked when a connection reports "your name came up in [Techstars / Antler / YC] Slack."

## Why it matters
Segment 3 metrics are the practitioner-credibility signal. They feed two things: (a) the credibility marker "advises X AI startups" that strengthens Segments 1 and 2 trust, and (b) X-channel reach via founder reshares to investors and other founders. Without Segment 3 traction, the practitioner positioning is theoretical.

## How to apply
- Track weekly: founder DMs received (separate from peer PM DMs), advisory inquiries logged, X engagement from named founder accounts, accelerator Slack mentions reported.
- Distinguish founder DMs from PM peer DMs in the tracker. They route to different follow-ups (founder = potential advisory; peer = potential reshare amplifier).
- Review monthly: which X threads pulled the strongest founder engagement? Patterns reveal which technical depth topics resonate.
- Maintain a quarterly count of active advisory relationships — this is the credibility number that goes on the speaker bio and the LinkedIn About section.

## Examples
- Q1 2026: 6 founder DMs, 3 from YC alumni, 1 advisory conversation that becomes a 6-month engagement. Logged in CRM with origin (X thread on retrieval architecture).
- An Antler alum quote-tweets Riché's X thread on context architecture. The quote tweet pulls 4 more founders into the conversation. One DMs about a specific retrieval problem; that becomes a 3-month informal advisory.
- A YC founder mentions Riché in their accelerator Slack: "If anyone is wrestling with context architecture, talk to Riché Zamor." Three founders DM in the next two weeks.

## Anti-patterns
- Counting every X engagement as Segment 3. Filter to founder bios; PM bios route to Segment 2.
- Treating advisory inquiries as immediate revenue. Most early advisory is informal value-building; equity follows after demonstrated impact.
