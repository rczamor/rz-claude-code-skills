# Segment 3 — Founders Building AI Products

## What it is
Seed to Series B AI founders — the technical/product founders shipping AI products who need architecture and product strategy advice. Many are accelerator alumni (YC, Techstars, Antler). They're X-native, not LinkedIn-native. They're in YC forums, founder Slacks, and on X talking to other founders. They're not the conversion target for Riché's hiring path, but they are a powerful flywheel input: founder advisory creates credibility that feeds Segments 1 and 2.

## Why it matters
Segment 3 generates two things Segments 1 and 2 respond to: (a) advisory inquiries that signal practitioner credibility ("Riché advises 4 AI startups on their context architecture"), and (b) founder reshares on X that reach an X-native audience including investors and other founders, some of whom intersect with Segment 1. Founders also turn into hiring managers — today's seed-stage founder is tomorrow's CPO at a Series B.

## How to apply
- Engage with founder content on X first (different platform mix than Segment 2). See the X target accounts in `corpus/growth/target-accounts/x.yaml`.
- Lean into the practitioner-not-theorist hook (see `corpus/growth/hooks/practitioner-not-theorist.md`). Founders don't trust theory; they trust people who've shipped.
- Use the live demo of the Context Layer Engine as the founder hook — it's the kind of artifact that makes a founder DM ask "can we talk?"
- Track Segment 3 reach via DMs asking for architecture advice, advisory inquiries, and X engagement from founder accounts.
- Be present in accelerator alumni networks (Techstars, Antler) where applicable.

## Examples
- A seed-stage YC founder DMs Riché after seeing a thread on context architecture: "Can you take a look at our retrieval setup?" That conversation turns into informal advisory; the founder reshares Riché's next post.
- An Antler alum founder mentions Riché in an accelerator Slack thread on AI product hiring. Two more founders DM. One becomes a long-term advisory relationship.
- A Series A founder in the X-native crowd quote-tweets Riché's thread with "this is the framework most AI PMs are missing." The reshare hits a founder/investor audience overlapping with Segment 1.

## Anti-patterns
- Treating Segment 3 like Segment 2 (LinkedIn-first). Founders live on X and in private Slacks, not on LinkedIn.
- Pitching for advisory equity early. Build trust through substance first; equity conversations follow naturally.
