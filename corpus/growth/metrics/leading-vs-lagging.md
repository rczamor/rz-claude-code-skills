# Leading vs. Lagging Indicators

## What it is
The distinction at the core of Riché's metrics system. Leading indicators predict future outcomes (you can act on them in real time); lagging indicators confirm past outcomes (you can only learn from them).

- **Leading indicators (weekly review):** comment quality, DM responses, reshares from 1K+ accounts, peer engagement on target accounts, profile views from AI companies.
- **Lagging indicators (quarterly review):** follower count growth, recruiter DMs received, advisory inquiries, speaking invitations accepted, job offers.

## Why it matters
Most personal-brand metrics dashboards over-weight lagging indicators (followers, impressions) and under-weight leading ones. The result: you only learn the strategy isn't working *after* a quarter has passed. Leading indicators let you steer mid-quarter — if reshares from 1K+ accounts are flat after 30 days of consistent posting, the bottleneck is Segment 2 relationships, not content. Adjust before another month wastes.

## How to apply
- **Track leading indicators weekly** (in the 15 min admin block). Update the dashboard from `corpus/growth/metrics/dashboard-template.md`.
- **Track lagging indicators quarterly.** Use the quarterly review checklist (`corpus/growth/metrics/quarterly-review-checklist.md`).
- **Don't conflate the two.** A bad week on leading indicators doesn't yet show in lagging indicators; a good week on lagging indicators may already have leading indicators softening.
- **Watch for divergence.** Strong leading + weak lagging = wait, the lag is normal (4–8 weeks). Weak leading + strong lagging = the past is paying out, but the next quarter is at risk.

## Examples
- Q1 2026 dashboard: reshares from 1K+ accounts up 40% (leading). Recruiter DMs flat (lagging). Diagnostic: leading is healthy; lagging will follow in Q2 with normal lag. Hold strategy.
- Q1 2026: reshares flat for 6 weeks (leading); but a podcast booking from December produced 2 inbound recruiter DMs (lagging). Diagnostic: lagging is paying out from prior quarter's work, but leading is signaling a slowdown. Diagnose Segment 2 gap before Q2.
- Mid-quarter: comment engagement from senior PMs spikes after a Wednesday deep dive (leading). Don't wait for the quarterly review — replicate that post's structure for the next 4 weeks.

## Anti-patterns
- Tracking only follower count (lagging, vanity). Tells you nothing actionable.
- Quarterly review without weekly leading-indicator tracking. By the time you notice the problem, you're a quarter behind.
