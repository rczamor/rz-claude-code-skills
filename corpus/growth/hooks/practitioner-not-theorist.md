# Practitioner, Not Theorist

## What it is
The first of the four differentiation pillars: Riché has shipped real AI products with real metrics. He's not a commentator, consultant, or "thought leader" who reads about AI from the sidelines. He built it; he ran it; he has the receipts. Specifically: Helm Labs ($3.25M pipeline, 300% growth, $0 CAC), Suzy (350+ enterprise customers, launch + growth phases), IBM AI Research (technical depth in production AI systems).

## Why it matters
The personal-brand AI/PM space is saturated with theorists — people who write think pieces, run cohorts, sell courses, but haven't shipped recently. Audiences are pattern-matched to spot the difference. Practitioners get reshared by other practitioners; theorists get clapped at by junior PMs. The practitioner positioning is what makes Segment 2 trust the substance and Segment 3 (founders) take the call.

## How to apply
- **Lead with practitioner credentials in every Segment 4 outreach.** LinkedIn About, speaker one-sheet, podcast pitches, CFP submissions all open with the credentials, not the credentials at the end.
- **Use specific metrics, not generic claims.** "$3.25M pipeline at Helm Labs" beats "led growth at multiple AI products." See `corpus/growth/hooks/specific-metrics-from-real-products.md`.
- **Reference shipping experience in posts.** Friday story content type is anchored on shipping anecdotes — Suzy launch context drift moment, Helm Labs pipeline mechanics. See `corpus/content-system/`.
- **Don't soften the credentials.** "Various AI products" is the wrong direction; specifics are the proof.

## Examples
- LinkedIn About: "I built a personal knowledge engine from scratch to prove that context architecture, not model intelligence, is the real moat in AI products. $3.25M pipeline at Helm Labs. 350+ enterprise customers at Suzy. IBM AI Research." Practitioner-first.
- A Wednesday deep dive on context architecture references the Suzy launch directly: "When we launched Suzy with 350+ enterprise customers, our early disengagement read like PMF failure. It was context drift. Different bug." Specifics anchor the abstraction.
- A founder DMs after seeing the "300% growth, $0 CAC at Helm Labs" credibility marker: "Can we book 30 min — your retrieval setup mapped to a problem we're stuck on." Practitioner credentials → advisory inquiry.
