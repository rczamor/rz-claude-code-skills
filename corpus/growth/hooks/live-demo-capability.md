# Live Demo Capability

## What it is
The second differentiation pillar: Riché can demo the Context Layer Engine live on stage. He built a personal knowledge engine from scratch — the kind of artifact most "AI product thought leaders" don't have. The live-demo capability is what turns the speaker hook from claim to proof in real time.

## Why it matters
Almost no other PM voice can demo a working AI system live. The demo is the differentiator that closes Segment 4 (event organizers) — they need to fill stages with content that's memorable and concrete, and a live AI demo from a PM is rare enough to stand out. The demo also backs up every Segment 1 conversion: when a hiring manager lands on richezamor.com after a peer reshare, the demo is the proof point that the practitioner positioning is real.

## How to apply
- **Pin the Context Layer Engine demo URL on every Segment 4 surface:** LinkedIn profile (Featured), speaker one-sheet, CFP submissions, podcast pitches.
- **Reference the live-demo capability in the speaker hook.** "I can demo it live on stage." See `corpus/growth/speaking/the-hook.md`.
- **Keep the demo current.** Every 60–90 days, walk through it as a visitor; if anything's broken or outdated, fix.
- **Use the demo in real talks.** ProductTank NYC, Mind the Product, AI Product Summit — when speaking, demo. Never just slides.
- **Pair with cognitive science backing** (`corpus/growth/hooks/cognitive-science-backing.md`) — the demo is the artifact; the backing is the substance.

## Examples
- ProductTank NYC organizer DMs after a Segment 2 peer mentioned Riché. The DM closes with "the demo at richezamor.com is what made me reach out — that's exactly the kind of practitioner content our audience wants." Demo did the work.
- A CFP submission to AI Product Summit references the live-demo capability in the abstract. The committee accepts on the strength of "few speakers can demo a real AI system; this one will." Demo as differentiator.
- A podcast pitch to Lenny's references the demo: "Suggest 45-min interview with optional 10-min live demo segment of the Context Layer Engine." Demo creates an additional booking incentive.

## Anti-patterns
- Letting the demo go stale. A broken demo is worse than no demo — it actively damages credibility.
- Burying the demo URL three clicks deep. Front and center; speaker pitches assume one click.
