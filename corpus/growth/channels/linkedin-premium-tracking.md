# LinkedIn Premium for Profile-View Tracking

## What it is
LinkedIn Premium ($60–80/month tier) unlocks profile-view tracking — the ability to see who viewed the profile in the last 90 days, including their company and role. Without Premium, profile views are anonymized after the first few. With Premium, every viewer's name and company are logged.

## Why it matters
Profile views are the primary leading indicator for Segment 1 (hiring managers) reach. Segment 1 doesn't engage with content publicly; they view profiles privately. Without Premium, the most actionable Segment 1 proxy is invisible. With Premium, weekly review reveals which AI companies' employees are scanning the profile — the strongest early signal that the flywheel is reaching Segment 1.

## How to apply
- **Subscribe to LinkedIn Premium.** Cost: ~$60–80/month. Returns: 10x via Segment 1 proxy visibility.
- **Weekly review (in the 15 min admin block):** open Profile views, scan for AI company employees. Log named viewers in the dashboard with company, role, and date.
- **Pattern recognition:** which posts preceded a spike in AI-company profile views? Likely the post was reshared into a hiring-manager feed.
- **Don't reach out cold.** A profile view is a signal to keep posting consistently and to optimize the profile, not a permission slip to DM. See `corpus/growth/segments/segment-1-targeting-rules.md`.
- **Cancel and re-subscribe if needed.** The Premium subscription can be paused if the budget is tight; one quarter of data is enough to validate the patterns.

## Examples
- Week of June 20: Wednesday deep dive on context architecture posted June 18. By Friday, Premium shows 3 profile views from AI company employees (one VP at a Series A AI startup, two senior PMs at Forbes AI 50 companies). Logged. Pattern: the deep dive reached Segment 1 via Segment 2 reshares within 48 hours.
- A profile view from a CPO at a Forbes AI 50 company is logged. No outreach. Two weeks later, that CPO's recruiter DMs Riché. The "how did you hear about me" trace confirms the path.
- Quarterly: 14 named AI-company profile views, 6 traced to specific reshares. Pattern: deep dives convert better than hot takes for Segment 1 reach.
