# Speaker One-Sheet and Bio Page

## What it is
Two assets on richezamor.com optimized for Segment 4 (event organizers, podcast hosts) when they're verifying credibility:

1. **Speaker one-sheet (PDF):** one-page download with hook, talk titles, sample audience takeaways, headshot, and credibility markers.
2. **Bio page (web):** the canonical bio with extended background, talk archive, video clips from past talks, and contact form.

## Why it matters
Segment 4's job is to receive pitches and verify speakers in 30 seconds. A LinkedIn profile is good for that, but a dedicated speaker one-sheet is faster, more shareable internally (organizers forward PDFs to their committees), and signals professional preparation. The bio page picks up where the one-sheet leaves off — when an organizer wants more context, the page is the depth.

## How to apply

**Speaker one-sheet (PDF) — single page:**
- Top: name, headshot, headline, 1-line hook.
- Middle: 3–4 talk titles with 2-line abstracts. Lead with "Context Architecture: The Real Moat in AI Products" featuring the live demo.
- Bottom: credibility markers (Helm Labs $3.25M pipeline, Suzy 350+ enterprise customers, IBM background), past speaking venues, video clip URL, contact info.

**Bio page (web):**
- Hook and positioning at top.
- Extended background: roles, products shipped, specific metrics.
- Talk archive with embedded clips.
- Speaker requirements (AV needs, travel, fee range).
- Contact form or direct email.

**Voice and visual:** Neural Architect palette per `corpus/brand-system/`. Voice rules per `corpus/voice/`.

## Examples
- A Mind the Product CFP committee member receives a pitch from Riché. Within 60 seconds, she's downloaded the one-sheet and skimmed the bio page. The Helm Labs $3.25M number plus the Context Layer Engine demo URL plus the IBM credential close the credibility check.
- A Lenny's Podcast booker forwards the one-sheet to Lenny's team Slack. The PDF format makes internal sharing trivial. Booking conversation moves to scheduling within a week.
- ProductTank NYC organizer asks if Riché has a one-sheet to attach to her speaker confirmation email. Yes; sent within 5 min.

## Anti-patterns
- No one-sheet at all. Forces organizer to dig through LinkedIn for credibility — friction kills conversion.
- One-sheet that's a dense wall of text. The format must be scannable in 30 seconds.
