# LinkedIn Profile Optimization

## What it is
The LinkedIn profile is the primary conversion surface for Segment 1 (hiring managers) — the page Segment 1 lands on after a peer reshare. Optimization means: headline, About, Featured/pinned posts, and Experience all lead with the differentiated context-architecture positioning, not generic PM language.

## Why it matters
Segment 1 spends ~30 seconds on a profile before deciding whether to investigate further (recruiter outreach, hiring conversation, save for later). In those 30 seconds, generic PM positioning loses every time. The differentiation hooks (practitioner not theorist, live demo capability, cognitive science backing, specific metrics) compress into the headline, the About, and the pinned content. Get those right and the profile converts; get them wrong and the entire flywheel ends in a cold profile.

## How to apply

**Headline:** lead with the positioning. Example shape: "AI Product Leader | Context Architecture | Built personal knowledge engine to prove context > model intelligence | $3.25M pipeline at Helm Labs."

**About section:** open with the hook ("I built a personal knowledge engine from scratch to prove that context architecture, not model intelligence, is the real moat in AI products"). Follow with practitioner credentials (Helm Labs $3.25M pipeline, Suzy 350+ enterprise customers, IBM background). Close with a specific call-to-action (link to Context Layer Engine demo, link to richezamor.com).

**Featured / pinned posts:** 3 pieces. Recommended:
1. The canonical "What is context architecture?" article or deep dive
2. The Context Layer Engine demo (or video clip from a talk)
3. The strongest credibility-marker post (Helm Labs $3.25M pipeline story, or Suzy launch retro)

**Experience:** lead each role with the differentiated impact, not the title responsibilities. Helm Labs entry: "$3.25M pipeline; 300% growth; $0 CAC" — specific.

**Profile photo:** professional, signals seniority. Background image: subtle Neural Architect palette per `corpus/brand-system/`.

## Examples
- Before: "Senior Product Manager passionate about AI." After: "AI Product Leader | Context Architecture | Built a personal knowledge engine to prove context architecture is the real AI moat. $3.25M pipeline at Helm Labs. 350+ enterprise customers at Suzy."
- A VP of Product lands on the profile from a Lenny reshare. In 30 seconds: hook, credibility, demo URL. Within 90 seconds, she's clicked the Context Layer Engine demo and the LinkedIn Featured deep dive. Profile conversion succeeded.
